Social Responsability

With each bicycle that is sold, our company reverts 1% of the total sale to donate to non-governmental companies. The companies we chose to donate are companies that work in reforestation, work with volunteers to plant trees in parks and green areas in cities. We also opted to revert part of our donations to non-governmental companies that clean the bicycle trails, thus being able to contribute to a better world and also to the greater comfort of our customers when they go to ride the bicycle trails around the city. As an initiative of our own company, once a month we gather our employees and customers who want to participate with us to clean up a bike trail around the city, and after cleaning we have a barbecue event for everyone.

Currently Sponsored Institutions:

  • Greenpeace
  • The Wilderness Society
  • Earth Share NC
  • We Plant it Forward
